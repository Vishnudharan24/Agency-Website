Email Marketing

  • Email Marketing is a digital marketing strategy that involves sending targeted emails to a list of subscribers or customers. It aims to nurture relationships, build brand loyalty, and drive conversions by delivering personalized and relevant content directly to recipients' inboxes. Email marketing can include promotional offers, newsletters, product updates, event invitations, and more. The key components include building an email list, segmenting subscribers based on demographics or behaviors, crafting compelling content, optimizing for mobile devices, and measuring performance through metrics like open rates, click-through rates, and conversions.
